About wr.labs

Bridging Gaming & Data Science

At wr.labs, we believe that competitive games are rich environments for testing and deploying advanced machine learning systems. Our goal is to extract telemetry data from competitive environments and model them using state-of-the-art architectures to uncover non-obvious relationships.

By focusing heavily on model calibration and low-latency inference, we develop estimates that don't just achieve high baseline accuracy but remain statistically reliable under fluid, unpredictable match dynamics.

Our Vision

We aim to make sophisticated game-state modeling accessible. Whether for telemetry feeds, player post-game reviews, or statistical research, our network provides clean, calibrated probability indexes that uncover non-obvious game-state relationships.
