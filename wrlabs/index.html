Deep Item Winrates

Looking at raw item win rates is inaccurate, as the win rate of an item is highly dependent on the state of the game when it is purchased.

wr.labs resolves this by utilizing machine learning to adjust for gold levels, game duration, and player coordinates at the frame of purchase, while looking at the effect of the item purchase on the result of the game.

Our modeling builds upon and improves academic foundations in MOBA winner prediction, specifically optimizing methodologies introduced by C. H. Ke et al. on team fight modeling and D.-H. Kim et al. on confidence-calibrated winner predictors.

Post-Training Calibration

In modeling complex game-state relationships, accuracy is only half the battle. A model must also be calibrated.

When wr.labs indicates a team has a 70% chance of winning, it means that across thousands of identical game states, the leading team will go on to win exactly 70% of those games.

We apply advanced mathematical calibration post-training to align our raw neural predictions with empirical match outcomes, minimizing error down to a tight 1–2% variance.
